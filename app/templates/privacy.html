1. Our Commitment

This policy is intended to align with Malaysia's Personal Data Protection Act 2010 (PDPA). We process personal data responsibly and only for legitimate security and service purposes.

We may collect information you provide (such as name, email, and account credentials), scan configuration data, and service usage logs required to deliver the platform.

3. Purpose and Consent

We use your data to operate the service, provide support, and improve security features. By using Perimeter and submitting data, you consent to this processing as required under the PDPA.

4. Disclosure

We do not sell personal data. We share data only with service providers necessary to operate the platform, or when required by law.

5. Security and Retention

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal data. We retain data only as long as necessary for service delivery, legal compliance, and auditability.

6. Cross-Border Transfers

Where data is transferred outside Malaysia, we ensure appropriate safeguards consistent with the PDPA.

7. Your Rights

Under the PDPA, you may request access to, correction of, or withdrawal of consent for the processing of your personal data.

8. Cookies and Analytics

We may use cookies or similar technologies to maintain sessions and improve user experience. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

9. Contact

For privacy requests or questions, contact privacy@perimeter.example.
